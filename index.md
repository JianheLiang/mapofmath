About 
The History of Math Knowledge Exchange
The Math Knowledge Exchange was born in 2025 from a simple idea: to create a space where mathematics lovers from around the world could connect, collaborate, and learn. Founded by a group of passionate students and educators, the site started as a small GitHub repository hosting problem sets and solutions.
Over time, it grew into a vibrant community. Key milestones include:

January 2025: Launched the initial website with a focus on algebra and calculus problems.
March 2025: Introduced discussion forums (planned feature) to encourage peer-to-peer learning.
June 2025: Expanded to include resources for geometry, number theory, and advanced topics, thanks to contributions from global volunteers.

Our mission remains to make mathematics accessible and engaging for all, fostering a culture of curiosity and collaboration.
